WHERE FOOD IS MAGIC

Food Feast strives to show that cuisines from Telangana and Andhra are not only about spice but beautiful, complex flavours.

Our chefs take age old traditional recipes, hand chosen spices, farm fresh products and along with years of experience serve food that will make you reminiscent of your mother's food.

We have added a new chapter to Food Feast - with our cloud kitchens. Through this new venture, we offer a plethora of flavours with the utmost hygiene standards. Indulge in some authentic Asian food flavours at Oriental Tales; taste the flavours of the pind at Punjabi Tales and find the all tome classic at Biryani Tales.
